BrokerageAccount

A Schema.org Type

This term is proposed for full integration into Schema.org, pending implementation feedback and adoption from applications and websites.
Thing > Intangible > Service > FinancialProduct > InvestmentOrDeposit > BrokerageAccount
  • Canonical URL: http://schema.org/BrokerageAccount
  • Leave public feedback on this term 💬
  • Check for open issues.
An account that allows an investor to deposit funds and place investment orders with a licensed broker or brokerage firm.
