MoneyTransfer

A Schema.org Type

This term is proposed for full integration into Schema.org, pending implementation feedback and adoption from applications and websites.
Thing > Action > TransferAction > MoneyTransfer
  • Canonical URL: http://schema.org/MoneyTransfer
  • Leave public feedback on this term 💬
  • Check for open issues.
The act of transferring money from one place to another place. This may occur electronically or physically.
